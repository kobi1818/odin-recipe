Southern EggPie

DESCRIPTION

This is a pseudo Southern take on a quiche.

Ingredients

  • 1 table spoon butter
  • 1/2 onion chopped
  • 4 eggs
  • Salad Dressing
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 8 ounce of Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 dash of hot pepper (OPTIONAL)
  • 1 pinch white sugar
  • unbaked dish pie crust

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F
  2. Heat butter in large skillet
  3. Whisk together all the Ingredients
  4. Spread the ingredients in the bottom crust
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 15 to 30 minutes
